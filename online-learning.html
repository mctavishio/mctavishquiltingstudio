Online learning opportunities

We have available a series of pre-recorded online classes. These rich tutorials provide you with access to captioned recorded content that you can explore on your own timeline. Karen shares her years of experience with you and offers hands-on demonstrations of skills that you can use in your own practice.

We are able to provide these videos to you through the generous donations of our sponsors. We would love to have you join this list!

Slow train series

Watch an example of the slow train series, above. This unnarrated series provides lengthy close-up shots that show the rhythm of Karen's work. Each film takes you deep into the process of quilting on a free-motion, longarm machine. The extended footage allows you to study Karen's approach up close.

APQS Ellie

Meet Ellie and our Halo Table.

This duo delivers big quality within our smallest footprint ever.

No giant quilting room? No problem. Ellie is APQS's smallest and most economical quilting machine. Her simple to learn and easy-to-use controls, combined with lightweight handling make her the perfect, economical quilting machine for any small space because she is perfectly paired with our Halo Table. You'll be able to use this dynamic duo in a spare bedroom or office. But even though this duo is petite, you'll have an actual quilting space of 53" wide and 15 1/2" deep. Along with Ellie's 18" of throat space, you can tackle any size project from small table runners to larger quilts. Ellie on the Halo Table creates the perfect quilting combination, allowing you to gain an incredible quilting experience backed by our lifetime warranty and customer support. Halo Table

The size of your quilt is no longer limited by the width of the frame. Our Halo Table delivers the perfect table frame for any size project. Sitting at only 5' 6" wide X 3' 8" deep, the table is easy to assemble and designed to make moving your quilt around a breeze.

Simply lay your basted quilt over the Halo frame and attach it with our easy-to-use clamps and begin quilting, moving the quilt and reclamping as you go.

Our custom track system comes standard on our Halo table allowing for smooth machine movement and the high-end feel of our larger models. You'll quilt with the ease that only APQS machines and tables deliver. Halo also features easy table height adjustment to allow for standing or seated quilting.

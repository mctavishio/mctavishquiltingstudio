APQS sales & service

McTavish Quilting Studio is an APQS showroom, offering sales, service and rentals. We can help you get the most out of your APQS longarm quilt machine with beginner classes and continued local support for your machine's lifetime warranty.

We help you get the most from your apqs experience

Karen McTavish has years of experience using these machines to produce award-winning quilts. She backs everything she sells with a lifetime of support and training. When you purchase an apqs longarm from McTavish Quilting Studio you get:

  • a lifetime warranty
  • personalized assistance in choosing the right machine for you
  • support directly from Karen McTavish, bringing you over 28 years of experience in the industry
  • assistance setting up your longarm
  • free, extensive beginner class
  • beginner to advanced computerized QuiltPath classes
  • beginner to advanced continuing education to help you improve your technical and design skills
  • financing packages
  • professional consulting to help you start your own quilting business
  • money spent at McTavish Quilting Studio towards longarm rental can be applied to your new longarm purchase for up to $1500 off
